MATICAN Polka Dot Paper Ice Cream Cups

Bring a burst of color and style to your dessert presentations with MATICAN's 50 reusable polka dot paper ice cream cups. Their eye-catching design and versatile function make them the ultimate choice for ice cream shops, concession stands, caterers, and even home use.
